    Cucumber Sandwich
    Estimated Calorie : 358cal

    Health Benefits: cucumbers can add to your hydration status. They contain the antioxidant silica, which plays an important role in collagen synthesis and keeping your skin smooth and firm. There is also evidence that suggests the compounds in cucumbers may help prevent cancer and diabetes.

    Ingredients 2 ounces cream cheese, at room temperature 1 tablespoon low-fat plain Greek yogurt 1 tablespoon sliced fresh chives 1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill ¼ teaspoon ground pepper 2 slices whole-wheat sandwich bread ⅓ cup thinly sliced English cucumber

    Method : Stir cream cheese, yogurt, chives, dill and pepper together in a small bowl until well blended. Spread the mixture evenly on one side of each bread slice. Top 1 slice with cucumber slices, then top with the other bread slice, cream cheese-side down. Cut the crusts from the sandwich and cut it in half diagonally.
