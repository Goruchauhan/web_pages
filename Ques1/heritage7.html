DESCRIPTION :

During our quest to find places to visit near Hyderabad, we found the Ghanpur Group of Temples. This was another bike trip, we stumbled upon this lesser-known Ghanpur Group of Temples. Honestly, despite planning the trip, we had absolutely no idea about these temples. Thanks to the board by Telangana Tourism, we thought of driving an extra 11 kms for a detour to the temple. Of course, we didn't regret it..
