CHERIAL TEMPLE


DESCRIPTION :

Cherial temple also located in a Temple is a 2,000 year-old Jain temple at the village of Kolanupaka in Aler City, Yadadri district, Telangana, India.The temple houses three deities: one each of Lord Rishabhanatha, Lord Neminatha, and Lord Mahavira. The image of Lord Rishabhanatha, carved of a green stone has been historically famous as "Manikyaswami" and Jivantasvami.The temple is about 80 km from Hyderabad on the Hyderabad-Warangal Highway NH 163.
